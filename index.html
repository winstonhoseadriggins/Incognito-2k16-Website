National Level Technical Symposium conducted by the Department of Information Technology

It is part of ConoScenza that is conducted every year, in order to bring together students from colleges across the nation in healthy competition. The sole purpose of the event is to provide a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and compete against other students. All work and no play makes jack a dull boy! So Incognito is going to be an all inclusive package for learning, fun, food and much more! We look forward to seeing you!
